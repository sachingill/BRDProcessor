# BRD 004 - Onboarding Automation

## Problem
New employee onboarding is slow and inconsistent across teams.

## Objectives
- Cut onboarding time by 50%
- Ensure 100% completion of mandatory steps

## Functional Requirements
- Trigger onboarding workflow on new hire
- Assign tasks to IT, HR, and managers
- Track completion status

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Audit logs for compliance
- 99% uptime

## Constraints
- Integrate with Workday
