# BRD 006 - Marketing Campaign Analyzer

## Problem
Campaign performance data is fragmented and hard to compare.

## Objectives
- Provide a unified campaign dashboard
- Improve ROI reporting accuracy

## Functional Requirements
- Ingest data from email, ads, and social platforms
- Normalize metrics and compare channels
- Export weekly performance reports

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Weekly batch processing
- Role-based permissions

## Constraints
- Use existing BI stack for visualization
