# BRD 002 - Inventory Forecasting

## Problem
Inventory stockouts and overstocking cause lost revenue and higher storage costs.

## Objectives
- Improve forecast accuracy by 20%
- Reduce stockouts by 30%

## Functional Requirements
- Ingest sales and inventory data
- Generate weekly demand forecasts
- Alert on low-stock risks

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Daily refresh
- 95% uptime

## Constraints
- Use existing ERP as source of truth
