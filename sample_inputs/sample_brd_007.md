# BRD 007 - Delivery ETA Predictor

## Problem
Customers receive inaccurate delivery ETAs.

## Objectives
- Improve ETA accuracy by 25%
- Reduce late delivery complaints

## Functional Requirements
- Ingest driver and route data
- Predict ETA per shipment
- Notify users on major ETA changes

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Update every 15 minutes
- 99.9% uptime

## Constraints
- Integrate with fleet management system
