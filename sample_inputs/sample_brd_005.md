# BRD 005 - Fraud Alerting

## Problem
Fraud alerts are delayed and lack prioritization.

## Objectives
- Detect high-risk transactions within 2 minutes
- Reduce false positives by 15%

## Functional Requirements
- Stream transaction data
- Score transactions in real time
- Alert fraud team with risk score

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Latency under 120 seconds
- 99.5% uptime

## Constraints
- Must integrate with existing SIEM
