# BRD 009 - Compliance Monitoring

## Problem
Policy violations are discovered too late in audits.

## Objectives
- Detect violations within 24 hours
- Provide compliance evidence reports

## Functional Requirements
- Monitor activity logs
- Flag policy violations
- Generate audit-ready reports

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Daily scans
- 99.5% uptime

## Constraints
- Retain logs for 1 year
