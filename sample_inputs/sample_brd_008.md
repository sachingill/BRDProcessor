# BRD 008 - Self-Service Analytics

## Problem
Business teams rely on engineering for ad-hoc reports.

## Objectives
- Enable self-service dashboards
- Reduce reporting requests by 60%

## Functional Requirements
- Provide dataset catalog
- Build drag-and-drop dashboard builder
- Schedule report exports

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Access controls by team
- 99% uptime

## Constraints
- Must use existing data warehouse
