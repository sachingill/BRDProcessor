# BRD 001 - Support Triage Automation

## Problem
Manual ticket triage is slow and inconsistent, causing delayed responses.

## Objectives
- Reduce time-to-acknowledge by 40%
- Auto-route tickets by severity
- Improve SLA reporting

## Functional Requirements
- Ingest tickets from email and web form
- Classify issue type and severity
- Route tickets to correct queue

## Non-Functional Requirements
- 99.9% uptime
- Handle 1,000 tickets/day

## Constraints
- Integrate with Zendesk
- Deploy on AWS
