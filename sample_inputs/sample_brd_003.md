# BRD 003 - Customer Feedback Insights

## Problem
Customer feedback is scattered across tools and not actionable.

## Objectives
- Centralize feedback into a single dashboard
- Identify top 5 recurring pain points monthly

## Functional Requirements
- Ingest reviews from app stores and surveys
- Classify feedback themes
- Provide trend and sentiment views

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Weekly updates
- Role-based access

## Constraints
- Data residency in EU
